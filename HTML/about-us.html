Get To Know Us A Little Better.


Welcome to Los Pollos Hermanos!

At Los Pollos Hermanos, we believe in the power of good food and family values. Founded by our beloved owner, Gustavo Fring, we pride ourselves on serving the finest fried chicken in New Mexico, crafted with the freshest ingredients and a secret recipe that keeps our customers coming back for more.


Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide a dining experience that brings joy and comfort to our community. We understand the importance of great food, and we are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and service. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or gathering with loved ones, our warm and welcoming atmosphere is designed to make you feel at home.


Our Story

Los Pollos Hermanos started as a small family business with a dream to create the ultimate fried chicken experience. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved staple in the community, known not just for our delicious food but also for our commitment to excellence. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every meal is prepared to perfection, reflecting our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.


The Experience

When you dine with us, you’ll be treated to a menu full of mouthwatering options, from our signature crispy fried chicken to our delectable sides and sauces. Each dish is made with care, ensuring that every bite is packed with flavor. We also offer catering services for your special events, making sure that your celebrations are unforgettable.


Join Us

We invite you to visit Los Pollos Hermanos and experience the taste that has made us a local favorite. Come for the chicken, stay for the friendly service, and enjoy the sense of community that makes us who we are. Thank you for being a part of our story—we look forward to serving you soon!
